Campus Life

Science Lab

Class 5 and beyond.

Science is different from any other subject that a student takes up in school. Science involves seeing, handling, understanding and manipulating of real objects and materials. It is found that combine classroom or textbook teaching with science laboratory experiments are able to derive the best results. Thus SIS have provided opportunities to its students to learn the various methods and processes by using scientific equipment.

School Library

Books are man's best friend.

The school library exists to provide a range of learning opportunities for both small groups as well as individuals to access and use quality information and resources, help them to enhance their study and research skills.

Won 3rd place in the InterSchool MCC
